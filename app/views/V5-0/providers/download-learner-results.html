Download T Level results for your learners

You can download the results for all of your active learners who are in the final year of their T Level.

The results will be available as a CSV file the day before each results day.

The results in the file may be updated after results day if:

  • a grade has changed due to a review or appeal
  • a learner has completed their industry placement

The file will update each time you download it. Any changes to results will be updated on the results file on the next working day.

The results file will not be updated after:

  • 31 May for overall results provided at the end of the autumn series
  • 31 October for overall results provided at the end of the summer series

Following these dates, you will need to look at individual learner records for up-to-date results.
