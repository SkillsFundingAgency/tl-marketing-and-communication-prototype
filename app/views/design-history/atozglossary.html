F

Further Education (FE)

This is an educational level between secondary and higher education. It tends to be vocational. Most people studying FE courses will be over 16, but some courses are available to people aged 14 and over. FE courses are usually taught in FE colleges, sixth form colleges, and adult education centres (ie not schools).
