Known Issues

These are issues that we are aware of in the prototype, but have decided that the priority to fix them is too low for the resources that it would take.

  • Filters do not work in conjunction with pagination. Changing to a new page will clear the current filters
  • Inputting maths, English, ip, or certificate changes on a learner will not remove the option to add this data again on the manage-t-learner-records page
  • Inputting maths, English, ip, or certificate changes on a learner and then doing the same to another learner will wipe the data from the previous learner
  • The success banner that appears when you change learner details will persist until you either clear your data, or add data to a new learner
