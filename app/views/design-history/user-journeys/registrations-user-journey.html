Awarding organisation - Registrations

User journey for AO's to inform ESFA which T levels they deliver

From: R&C Team

Published: 25 January 2022

Last updated: 25 January 2022

Journey overview

Awarding organisation's inform ESFA of the details of T level learners.

Information collected includes:

  • Learner name
  • Date of birth
  • Provider and UKPRN
  • T level title
  • Core
  • Occupational specialism if known at time of registration
  • Academic year

End users

  • Registrations Editor (user role in the service)
  • Administrators

Pre requisites before adding registrations

  • AO must have reviewed the T Levels they offer within the R&C service
  • AO must have reviewed which providers will offer their T Levels
  • Providers send across the registration details to the AO of the students
  • AO prepare the registration information for their own internal system and for bulk upload to R&C service (spreadsheet template provided by us)

Current Functionality

  • Users are able to bulk upload of up to 10000 registrations in a single CSV file.
  • Template provided to users for required data format - (Template is provided as a XLSX format not CSV).
  • Export registrations data via a CSV download.
  • Manage an individual learner record
