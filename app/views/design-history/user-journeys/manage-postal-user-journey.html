Providers - Manage your organisations postal address

User journey for Providers to inform ESFA the address of their school or college where certificates and statement of achievements will be sent to.

From: R&C Team

Published: 25 January 2022

Last updated: 25 January 2022

Journey overview

Providers provide ESFA the address of their school/college where certificates and statement of achievements will be sent to.

Providers provide this information either via a postcode and selecting an address or entering the address manually.

Providers MUST provide an address before a statement of achievement can be requested.

End users

  • Learner records editor (user role in the service)
  • Administrators

Pre requisites before requesting a statement of achievement

  • No pre-requisites needed before

Current Functionality

Searching for an address via postcode

Add address manually:

  • Department
  • Organisations name
  • Building and street
  • Town or City
  • Postcode

No address found for inputted postcode

Change organisation address (once its been added)

Cancel adding or changing an address
