Awarding organisation - Appeals

User journey for AO's to inform ESFA of which learners have requested an appeal and the outcome of the appeal.

From: R&C Team

Published: 25 January 2022

Last updated: 25 January 2022

Journey overview

AO's inform ESFA of which learners have appealed and what the outcome of the appeal was.

Information provided:

  • Which component the learner has taken is being appealed
  • Outcome of appeal - the final grade

End users

  • Results Editor (user role in the service)
  • Administrators

Pre requisites before adding results

  • AO must have reviewed the T Levels they offer within the R&C service.
  • AO must have reviewed which providers will offer their T Levels .
  • AO must have registered the learners from their providers.
  • Needs to have a current assessment entry to be registered against the learners record.
  • AO must have provided a result for the above assessment entry within the R&C service.

Current Functionality

Users inform us if a learners core component is being appealed on behalf of learner on an individual basis through searching for the learners ULN

Users inform ESFA the outcome of an appeal:

  • Whether the grade has stayed the same
  • Whether the grade has changed
  • Learner has withdrawn (or it was added by mistake)

Additional Scenarios catered for:

  • Learner ULN not found
  • Learner has been withdrawn
  • Learner does not have any results recorded
  • Learner does not have any registrations registered
  • Request a grade change if user has inputted an incorrect appeal outcome
  • Inform us of a learners request for an appeal after the deadline
