Providers - Manage learner records

User journey for Providers to inform ESFA of industry placement status, maths and english attainment level for learners.

From: R&C Team

Published: 25 January 2022

Last updated: 25 January 2022

Journey overview

Add a T Level record

Providers send registrations details of the learners to the Awarding Organisation (AO).

The learners are then registered into the R&C service (ESFA) by the AO.

Once the learners is registered into the service, the provider is able to add a 'new T Level record' in the R&C service (ESFA) to provide information of the individual learners:

  • Industry Placement status
  • Maths and English status

Update a T Level Record

After creating a T Level record for the learner, the user is able to update the:

  • Industry Placement status
  • Maths and English status

The user is able to amend/update this information throughout the learners time on the T Level course.

End users

  • Learner records editor (user role in the service)
  • Administrators

Pre requisites before managing a T Level record

  • Provider must have sent the AO the registration details of the learners.
  • AO must have registered the learners with ESFA via the R&C service.
  • Add a T Level record - Must not have created a record beforehand.
  • Update a T level record - Must have created a record beforehand.

Current Functionality

Add a new T Level record and update a T Level record on individual basis.

Scenarios catered for:

  • Learner not registered by awarding organisation
  • Learners without LRS data
  • Learners with LRS data
  • Provider has not created a T Level record for the learner in the system when searching for the ULN in the update T Level record journey - user will be entered into the 'Add a T level record'
  • Provider has already a created T Level record when trying to search for a ULN in the 'add a T Level record' - The user will be entered into the update a T level record journey
