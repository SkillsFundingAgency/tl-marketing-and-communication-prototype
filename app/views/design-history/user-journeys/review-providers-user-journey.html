Awarding organisation - Review providers

User journey for AO's to inform ESFA which providers are offering the T Levels that the AO delivers

From: R&C Team

Published: 25 January 2022

Last updated: 25 January 2022

Journey overview

Awarding organisations will need to inform ESFA through the service of which providers will be delivering which T Levels

Once completed, this task will be done on an ad-hoc basis as and when the awarding organisation is notified of any changes in which providers will be:

  • starting new T Levels
  • stopping the delivery of T Levels or
  • a new provider will be delivering T Levels

End users

  • T Level Editors (user role in the service)
  • Administrators

Pre requisites before reviewing providers

  • Must have reviewed the T Levels they offer within the R&C service

Current Functionality

Users are able to:

  • Review existing providers
  • Add or remove providers
  • Select the T Levels each provider offers
