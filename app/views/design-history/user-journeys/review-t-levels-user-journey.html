Awarding organisation - Review T Levels

User journey for AO's to inform ESFA which T levels they deliver

From: R&C Team

Published: 25 January 2022

Last updated: 25 January 2022

Journey overview

Awarding organisations first task when interacting with the service is to confirm which T Levels they will be delivering.

Once completed, this task will be done on an ad-hoc basis as and when the awarding organisation adds or stops delivering T Levels.

End users

  • T Level Editors (user role in the service)
  • Administrators

Pre requisites before reviewing T levels

  • None - first step for users to complete

Current Functionality

  • Users confirm on an individual basis the list of T Levels and details we hold for their Awarding Organisation.
  • Users are able to query any of the above T Levels if the details are incorrect.
  • Users are able to see all of the T Levels they have confirmed are correct.
