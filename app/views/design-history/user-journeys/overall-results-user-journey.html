Providers - Manage learner records

User journey for Providers to inform ESFA of industry placement status, maths and english attainment level and view overall T Level grades for learners.

From: R&C Team

Published: 25 January 2022

Last updated: 25 January 2022

Journey overview

Providers send registrations details of the learners to the Awarding Organisation (AO).

The learners are then registered into the R&C service (ESFA) by the AO.

Once the learners is registered into the service, the provider is able to view the learners record in the R&C service (ESFA) including learners overall T Level grade.

As well as provide information on:

  • Industry Placement status
  • Maths and English status

End users

  • Learner records editor (user role in the service)
  • Administrators

Pre requisites before managing learner record

  • Provider must have sent the AO the registration details of the learners.
  • AO must have registered the learners with ESFA via the R&C service.

Current Functionality

Users are able to search for learners via learner name or ULN.

Users can filter and search results in parallel.

Users are able to sort results via date updated or last name.

Users are able to export learner records including records that have been filtered.

Within each of the individual learner records users are able to add:

  • Industry placement status
  • Maths attainment level
  • English attainment level
