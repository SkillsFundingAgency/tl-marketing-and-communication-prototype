Manage T Level results

This service provides T Level results, certificates and statements of achievement for your learners.

You must give us:

  • industry placement details for each learner
  • missing English and maths details
  • your organisation's postal address

Check what data you need to provide and when you need to provide it.

View an example T Level results file (opens in a new tab).

In this section

  • Manage learner records

    Add and edit achievements and industry placement statuses. You can also order replacement certificates and statements of achievement.

  • Download learner results

    Download results for active learners in the final year of their T Level.

  • Upload industry placement data

    Upload a CSV file to add or change the industry placement status for one or more learners.

  • Request statement of achievement for withdrawn learners

    Request a new or replacement statement of achievement for learners who have withdrawn from their T Level course.

  • Manage your organisation's postal address

    Add or update the address you want certificates and statements of achievement sent to.

  • Manage your account

    View your account profile and permissions.
