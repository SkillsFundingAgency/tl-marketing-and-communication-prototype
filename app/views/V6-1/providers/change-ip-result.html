What is special consideration?

Learners may experience difficult circumstances during their placement, which mean they are unable to complete the minimum placement hours before the end of the second year.

You have the discretion to apply special consideration in some exceptional circumstances where students have demonstrated sufficient progress towards their learning goals but have not completed the minimum placement hours.

You do not need to upload any evidence, but you should hold supporting documentation in your own files.
