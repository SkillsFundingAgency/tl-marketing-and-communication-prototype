Manage T Level results

Use this service to tell us about:

  • the T Levels you deliver and which providers are offering them
  • your registered learners, when they will be assessed and the results they achieve

The information you give us will be used to calculate learners' overall grades and printed on their certificates.

Navigation

  • T Levels

    Review, change and confirm the T Levels you offer.

  • Providers

    Manage providers and select the T Levels they can offer.

  • Registrations

    Register, edit and withdraw learners, and download registrations data.

  • Assessments

    Tell us when your learners will be assessed and download assessments data.

  • Results

    Tell us the initial grades learners achieved and download results data.

  • Reviews, appeals and grade changes

    Tell us about any reviews of marking and moderation (RoMMs) or appeals and report their outcomes.

  • Account

    View and edit your profile. If you're an administrator, you can also manage other users' accounts.
