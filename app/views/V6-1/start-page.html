Manage T Level results

This service is for awarding organisations and education providers who deliver T Level qualifications. It is managed and run by the Department for Education (DfE).

Awarding organisations can use this service to manage T Level:

  • course details
  • learners
  • results

Education providers can use this service to:

  • download T Level results
  • manage learners' maths, english and industry placement details
  • request replacement certificates and statements of achievement
Start now

Before you start

You will need a DfE Sign-in account and at least one assigned role. Read more information about DfE Sign-in roles for manage T Level results.

This service allows you to bulk upload specific learner information using templates. Find out more about T Levels data formats, rules and templates.
