Tell us if a grade is being reviewed or appealed

You must tell us:

  • that a learner has requested a review of marking or moderation (ROMM)
  • the outcome of any ROMM
  • that a learner has requested an appeal
  • the outcome of any appeal

This is to make sure that the learner does not receive:

  • an overall T Level result that has been calculated using a grade that is not yet final
  • a T Level certificate with the incorrect overall result

Before you can tell us about any ROMM or appeal the learner must have:

  • an active registration (not withdrawn)
  • a recorded grade already in this system.
