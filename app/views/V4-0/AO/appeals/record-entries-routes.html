More about grades and their statuses
The highest grade for each component will be used to calculate the overall T Level result for results day. All grades must be marked ‘FINAL’ before they can be used to calculate an overall T Level result for certificate printing.Grades are marked ‘FINAL’ and can no longer be changed here when: the appeal deadline has passed, or an appeal outcome has been logged If a grade status is ‘ROMM’ or ‘APPEAL’ it cannot be used to calculate a T Level result for certificate printing until an outcome has also been logged.
