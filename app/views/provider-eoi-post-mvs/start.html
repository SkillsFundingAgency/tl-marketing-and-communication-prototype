Connect with employers
interested in T Levels

If you’re a T Level provider, use this service to find employers who have expressed interest in T Levels and industry placements.

Make sure employers can find you
Remember to check that your organisation’s details are up to date in the National Careers Service Course Directory so that potential employers and students can find you using our T Level provider search tools.

Before you start

You will need to sign in with your DfE Sign-in account and add this service to your account before you can use it.

How to create a DfE Sign-in account and add this service

Create a DfE Sign-in account

  1. Create a DfE Sign-in account.
  2. Add your organisation to your account.
  3. Wait for your organisation’s approver to grant you access.

Add this service to your DfE Sign-in account

  1. Sign into your account and go to 'Services'.
  2. Select 'Request access to a service' from the 'Related actions' list.
  3. Select ‘T Levels Connect with Employers’.
  4. Wait for approval from your organisation’s approver.
  5. This service will now be visible on the services dashboard, ready to use.

If you’re an approver, you can find more help on the T Levels support for schools and colleges site.
