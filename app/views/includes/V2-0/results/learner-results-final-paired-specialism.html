More about grade statuses
An overall T Level result cannot be calculated unless all assessments have grades marked 'final'.

If a result is marked as ‘pending’ the grade cannot be changed here. A review or an appeal outcome must be entered in the reviews and appeals section of this service.

Once a grade has been entered after a review or an appeal it is marked as ‘final’ and can no longer be changed here. If you need to change a grade after entering a review or an appeal result, or change a grade after the deadline, please contact us.
