Manage T Level results

On Monday 15 November, at the Second Reading of the Skills Bill, the Secretary of State announced changes to the English and maths requirements for T Levels. We have removed the exit requirement with immediate effect for all T Level students.

  • You must still provide us with English and maths attainment information.
  • The wording in this service has not yet been updated to reflect the policy changes.

More about the changes to T Level exit requirements and the timeline for level 3 qualifications reform (opens in a new window or tab).

Providers, for example, schools and colleges, can use this service to record a T Level learner's English and maths achievements and industry placement completion. These records will be added to the learner's T Level registration.

Exam boards, also known as awarding organisations, are responsible for registering each learner onto a T Level course and recording their technical qualification results.
