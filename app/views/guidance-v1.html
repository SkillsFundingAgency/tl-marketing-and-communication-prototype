About manage T Level results

Manage T Level results is the service that exam boards and education providers use to provide the Department for Education with T Level data. The department uses this data to calculate overall T Level grades and issue certificates.

Tasks you can complete here include:

  • adding learner information, including industry placement, maths and English details, if missing
  • requesting statements of achievement for withdrawn learners
  • downloading T Level results on results day

Adding learner information

You can add learner information manually, which means adding details of learners one at a time into this service.

Industry placements and special consideration

Learners may experience exceptional and adverse circumstances during their placement, which means they are unable to complete the minimum placement hours before the end of the second year.

You have the discretion to apply special consideration in some exceptional circumstances. These are where learners have demonstrated sufficient progress towards their learning goals and worked directly with an external employer, but have not completed the minimum placement hours.

We will ask you for the total number of hours completed and the reason for granting special consideration. You should hold supporting documentation in your own files.

Find out more about industry placement completion exceptions and special consideration.

Requesting statements of achievement

Statements of achievement are used to show which T Level components a learner has completed.

Learners are entitled to a statement of achievement if they have:

  • not achieved all their T Level components
  • withdrawn from their T Level course

Find out more about requesting a statement of achievement.

Downloading T Level results

The Department for Education publishes T Level results in CSV format to coincide with the publication of A level results.
