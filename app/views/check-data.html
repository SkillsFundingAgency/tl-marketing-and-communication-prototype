Manage T Level results timeline

Sunday 31 July, 2022
Industry placement T Level grade deadline

You must tell us whether a learner has completed their industry placement by 31 July for us to be able to calculate an overall T Level grade for results day.

You can tell us this information as soon as you have it.

Find out more about the industry placement information we will ask you for.

Wednesday 17 August, 2022
T Level results issued

We publish the results for 2020 to 2021 learners who have completed all T Levels components, ready for results day on 18 August.

Monday 31 October, 2022
Industry placement certificate deadline

You must tell us any outstanding industry placement information by 31 October for us to be able to issue T Level certificates to learners.

Monday 31 October, 2022
English and maths certificate deadline

For most learners, we get English and maths information from the LRS. You must tell us any missing English and maths information by 31 October for us to be able to issue T Level certificates to learners with the correct information.
